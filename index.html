The Rules

To start the game, select easy or hard level above and press button "Start". Try to fins all the pairs by clicking on the card to show its face and so on.
If the cards do not match, the cards will shake and flip back to face down. You win the game if you find all the pairs before the timer hits 00:00 !
If you wish to know more about memory card games then click on 'About Us' in the menu on top of the page.
If you would like to go back to the home page, simply click on "Home".
Have fun and good luck!
